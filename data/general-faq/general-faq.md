# Introduction — About Morgan Treasuries

Morgan Treasuries Limited is owned by Bluestone 1 (Pvt) Limited, with strong representation on the Board, led by Mr. Ajit Gunewardene as Chairman, alongside Mr. Ronnie Peiris and Mr. Shantanu Nagpal. These industry veterans bring with them extensive and well-established track records, guiding Morgan's strategic vision and growth.

Morgan, formerly known as Melsta Regal Finance Ltd, was a fully owned subsidiary of Melstacorp PLC. The transition in ownership from a reputable Sri Lankan conglomerate to Bluestone 1 (Pvt) Limited, a trusted multinational, has reinforced Morgan's strength and resilience, empowering the company to pursue its long-term corporate objectives.

The brand name "Morgan" is derived from the words 'financial entrepreneurs' and 'experts', underscoring the powerful synergy of innovation and expertise that distinguishes the company from its competitors. The new brand identity embodies Morgan's drive to maximize potential and unlock value for its clients. It reflects a dynamic focus on action, agility, and speed in a fast-evolving marketplace.

Morgan's leadership prioritizes adopting best practices across the industry and places a significant emphasis on employee development. Through comprehensive training and skills enhancement programs, Morgan equips its team with the technical knowledge and competencies required to excel in a dynamic, highly competitive environment.

Looking ahead, Morgan's strategic roadmap is centered around embracing cutting-edge technology to enhance operational efficiency and align with the evolving transaction trends and expectations of modern clients. As part of this digital transformation, Morgan is also committed to minimizing its environmental footprint by ensuring its operations are sustainable, reducing reliance on natural resources through innovative solutions.

# Board of Directors

## 1. Ajit Gunewardene (Chairman)

Among Sri Lanka's most renowned business leaders, Mr Gunewardene's career has significantly shaped Sri Lanka's corporate sphere, particularly through his leadership at John Keells Holdings PLC (JKH), where he was pivotal in growing the company's market capitalization from LKR 1 billion in 1992 to LKR 190 billion in 2016. Over his 34-year tenure, his strategic focus spanned real estate, leisure, financial services, and retail—securing market leadership positions across these sectors.

## 2. Ronnie Peiris (Non-Independent, Non-Executive Director)

Mr. Peiris, until the end of December 2017, served as an Executive Director on the Board of John Keells Holdings PLC (JKH) and was its Group Finance Director. In this capacity, he held directorships across multiple listed and non-listed companies spanning a wide array of industries, including leisure and hoteliering, food and beverage manufacturing and retailing, financial services, property development, information technology, plantations, and transportation.

## 3. Shantanu Nagpal (Non-Independent, Non-Executive Director)

Mr. Shantanu Nagpal, Co-Founder of Bluestone, brings two decades of experience in asset management and equity research across Hong Kong and London before relocating to Sri Lanka. Over the course of his career, he has held key roles as a portfolio manager at renowned firms such as UBS Asset Management, Ellerston Asset Management, and Brevan Howard Asset Management.

## 4. Ahamed Sabry Ibrahim (Independent Non-Executive Director)

Mr. Ahamed Sabry Ibrahim was appointed as a Director of Morgan Treasuries Limited on June 14, 2021. Before joining Morgan, he served as the Chief Executive Officer and General Manager at People's Leasing and Finance PLC.

He holds an Honours Degree (B.Sc) from the University of Colombo and is a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Bankers – UK (FCIB), further emphasizing his extensive qualifications in the field.

## 5. Shrihan B. Perera (Independent Non-Executive Director)

Mr. Shrihan Perera holds a B.Sc. (Honors) degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Moratuwa and is a Fellow Member of the Chartered Institute of Management Accountants (CIMA), UK, earning the CGMA designation. He was appointed CEO of the Teejay Group in May 2018, bringing with him over a decade of experience in the apparel industry.

## 6. Nilam Jayasinghe (Independent, Non-Executive Director)

Mr. Nilam Jayasinghe was appointed as a Director of Morgan Treasuries Ltd on October 1, 2021. Currently working as an Independent Consultant, he previously held the position of Group Director Finance at the CBL Group (Ceylon Biscuits) and served as an Executive Director at CBL Investments Limited, along with several other subsidiaries, from 2012 until his retirement in July 2021.

## 7. Fonny De Fonseka (Independent Non-Executive Director)

Mr. Fonny De Fonseka brings over 30 years of extensive experience in the field of Information Technology. He has held prominent positions, including Director at John Keells Software Technologies and Assistant Vice President of the Group IT Division, before joining MAS Holdings, where he led the IT function for the swimwear cluster.
