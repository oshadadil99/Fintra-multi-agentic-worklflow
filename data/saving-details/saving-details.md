# Morgan Vishwasa Fixed Deposits

Vishwasa Fixed Deposits present an excellent opportunity for individuals seeking to bolster their financial security while earning attractive returns. Meticulously designed to prioritise the safety of your investments, these fixed deposits allow you to invest a minimum of Rs. 5,000, thereby making it accessible to a wide audience. At Vishwasa, we understand that each customer has unique financial goals, which is why our offerings include a range of flexible deposit options tailored to meet varied preferences and timelines.

Vishwasa Fixed Deposits are available in terms ranging from 1 month all the way to 48 months, catering to both short-term and longer-term financial planning. This flexibility makes it easier for savers to confidently earmark funds for specific projects, education plans, or future expenses.

Deposits that accrue interest at maturity, as well as options for monthly and annual interest payments.

## Fixed Deposit Benefits

- Compelling Interest Rates
- Customers have the option to borrow up to 90% of the fixed deposit value through a loan.
- Selecting a Beneficiary Option
- Tailored Financial Guidance
- The capacity to establish joint deposit accounts.

## Fixed Deposit Eligibility

- Be a Sri Lankan National over 18 years holding a valid National Identity Card/Driving License.
